Our Mission

Monteforte Partners act as the Local Investment Operating Partner for institutional investors seeking opportunities in Italy.

We are based in London and Milan, thus we are able to provide unique access across the Italian SMEs landscape. We bring deep local expertise, tailored deal sourcing and structuring. We facilitate, and advise on Private Credit, Private Equity and Commercial Real Estate Investments.

Our Capabilities

Services

Monteforte Partners combine European institutional investor insight with deep local industry expertise to support complex investment decisions in Italy. We source deals through a blended multi-channel approach spanning local banks, credit agents, intermediaries and sector professionals.

Target Sectors in Italy

Our team brings extensive knowledge of key Italian economic sectors, with a focus on: Commercial Real Estate & Hospitality, Luxury & Consumer, Tech & B2B Services, Industrials & Infrastructure and Food & Agri.

Private Credit Solutions

We deliver access to a range of transformative business opportunities and bespoke financing solutions across the capital structure of Italian SMEs. We facilitate stable mid-long term “Patient” capital that complements traditional bank lending, including partnerships with local banks: Senior Secured Lending, Syndicated Bank Facilities, Asset-Backed Lending.

Private Equity Solutions

We provide equity capital opportunities in Italian SMEs: Growth Equity, Minority Investments, Hybrid Capital and Club Deals.

Senior Team

Monteforte Partners is led by seasoned professionals with multi-year cross-sector experience and a proven track record in Banking, ECM, Private Credit, Private Equity and Industrial Advisory.
